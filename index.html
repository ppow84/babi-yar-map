The March to Babi Yar

Kyiv · September 29, 1941 · Route reconstruction with Hähle photographs, Luftwaffe aerials & primary documents
“The older people who had difficulty walking were sighing in a barely audible way, but they silently continued their path of sorrow…”
— Dina Pronicheva, testimony, April 24, 1946
Johannes Hähle — Agfacolor Slides
September/October 1941 · 29 surviving slides · Archives of the Hamburg Institute for Social Research
Hähle, a Wehrmacht propaganda photographer, documented the streets of Kyiv, the march, and the aftermath at Babi Yar on Agfacolor reversal film. These are among the only known colour photographs of the events.
“The shooting of the Jews is a fact. It is this fact from which we are all beginning to lose our minds.”
— Iryna Khoroshunova, diary, October 2, 1941
