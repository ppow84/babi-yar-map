# Ereignismeldungen UdSSR (Nr. 1–195) — Online Sources Index
## Einsatzgruppen Operational Situation Reports | June 23, 1941 – April 24, 1942
### Compiled April 13, 2026

---

## 1. COMPLETE ORIGINAL GERMAN SCANS (ALL 195 REPORTS)

### Arolsen Archives (formerly International Tracing Service)
- **URL:** https://collections.arolsen-archives.org/de/archive/1-2-7-20_2273000/
- **Content:** 2,990 digitized page images of the original Ereignismeldungen UdSSR Nr. 1–195
- **Source archives:** Bundesarchiv Koblenz
- **Date range:** June 23, 1941 – April 24, 1942
- **Access:** Free, browsable online. Individual pages viewable by DocID.
- **Language:** German originals only (no translations)
- **EHRI portal entry:** https://portal.ehri-project.eu/units/de-002409-de_its_1_2_7-20-2273000

This is the **most complete freely accessible digital collection** of the original documents.

- Nr. 101 (Oct 2, 1941) — First report of 33,771 figure

## 6. PHYSICAL ARCHIVES WITH COMPLETE HOLDINGS

### National Archives and Records Administration (NARA), Washington DC
- **Record Group:** RG 242 (Captured German Records)
- **Microfilm:** Publication T-175, Records of the Reich Leader of the SS and Chief of the German Police (Reichsführer SS)
- **Content:** Complete original German documents on microfilm

### Bundesarchiv, Koblenz/Berlin
- **Bestand:** R 58/218 (and adjacent files)
- **Content:** Original documents returned from NARA in 1960
- **Digitized subset:** Available via Arolsen Archives (see above)

### Yad Vashem, Jerusalem
- **Content:** Complete photocopies of all reports from NARA microfilm

### USHMM, Washington DC
- **Collections search:** https://collections.ushmm.org/search/catalog/irn503291
- **Content:** Film copies and related documentation
